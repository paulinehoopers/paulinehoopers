About


For us, basketball is a very simple game. As an NBA player, Kyrie Irving, has always said, “The game of basketball has and will always be about getting buckets.” That statement means more to us than anything because that is another reason why we love this game. There’s nothing better than hearing the crowd roar after you make a defender look silly and then embarrass them by draining a shot in their face. Hitting a game-winning shot against a rival school, a game that many believed we weren’t supposed to win. Its an escape from stress, disappointment, bad grades, yelling parents, fake friends. Basketball has always been there for us. It doesn’t judge you. It doesn’t test you. It doesn’t give you a false sense of reality. It doesn’t feel sorry for you. It just waits for you. Always available. Always open. Always ready for you to come in and release whatever feelings you have. This is why we love basketball.
